About Kukulya

We are a locally owned landscaping company focused on craftsmanship, clear communication, and landscapes that age gracefully.

Our story

Kukulya started as a small landscaping business and grew into a full-service outdoor team. Along the way we kept the same rule: We promise on what we can deliver.

Today we take on design and install work, plus ongoing maintenance programs for homeowners, property managers, and small businesses who want one reliable partner for the outdoors.

Kukulya is also a volunteer firefighter—serving the same communities we work in, with the same commitment to showing up when people are counting on you.

How we work

Listen first

We start with how you use your yard—pets, kids, entertaining, parking—and what has frustrated you in the past.

Plan in writing

Scope, timeline, and goals are documented so everyone knows what success looks like.

Leave it neat
